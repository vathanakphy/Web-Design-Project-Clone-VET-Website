VET Background

Vireak Buntham Express Co., Ltd (VET) is a leading transportation and logistics company with a number of offices spread across Cambodia. Founded in 2004 by Neak Oknha Suo Vireak and registered as a company in 2006 at Koh Kong Province, VET provides transportation and logistics services crossing Cambodia, Thailand, and Vietnam. VET utilizes modern technology to deliver exceptional services.
